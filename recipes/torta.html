La torta mexicana


A torta is a sandwich served on a roll or bun. Torta’s can come in many varieties and can be served both hot or cold.
Your torta mexicana can be filled with a wide variety of ingredients including different types of meats and cheeses with beans, avocado, peppers, etc.

Ingredients:

  • Torta: cut in half and toasted
  • Mayonnaise
  • Refried beans
  • Avocado:mashed
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Thinly breaded, crispy chicken
  • Pickled jalapeños

  1. Prepare the chicken. Pound the chicken breast halves until thin. I like to put the chicken between two pieces
    of plastic wrap, and use a meat mallet or a rolling pin to gently pound it thin. Dip the chicken in beaten egg and then breadcrumbs.
  2. Cook the chicken.Cook the breaded chicken in a pan with hot oil until lightly browned on both sides, and cooked through.
  3. Assemble the torta.Slice the ciabatta roll in half and toast it. Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise, refried beans, and
    mashed avocado on the top piece of the roll. Layer the bottom part of the roll with the cooked chicken, cheese and peppers. Sandwich the roll together, and enjoy!
